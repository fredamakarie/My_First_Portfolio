The most populous

The wildebeest is the most populous wild animal in Africa. This is paricularly intersting for me as I have not yet had the pleasure of meeting one.

The most endangered

The black rhino has been listed as endangered since 2015. A little progress has been made to increase the numbers but there's still a lot of work to be done.
